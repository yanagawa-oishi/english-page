| English course International Communication Center

Adopting of language skills, cosmopolitan thinking and self-expression.

“I want to study abroad.” and “I want to use real English.”
The department is for students from many different countries to come to study with us to make their dreams come true.
The classroom is a microcosm of the world. Here is the door to the future.

Features of International communication center

  • "About half of the class are international students
    A natural way to improve your cosmopolitan thinking"
    In addition to international students from Asia, Europe and the United States, we also welcome graduates from our Thai junior high school. Students from different cultures and backgrounds interact with each other on a daily basis, which naturally helps them to develop the cosmopolitan thinking they will need in the future.

  • "Up to one year in the second year
    Study abroad without having to take a leave of absence"
    In the second year, all students study abroad. Students can choose to study abroad at one of our English-speaking partner schools, from short term to one year long. We also offer classes with English teachers and TOEFL and TOEIC practice (proficiency) classes to help students gain qualifications.

  • "Organise student-led events
    Improving and developing self-expression"
    The International communication center is a place where students can develop a sense of responsibility and action through student-led events, such as the World High School Student Summit to develop global leaders who can play an active role on the world stage.

About International Communication Center

Messages from a student and an English teacher

English course 2nd grade Seima Inoue(Ryujo Junior high school)
I decided to enter the International communication center because I admired the learning environment where I could study together with international students. I was worried about the impact of Corona, but remote learning started as soon as possible, and international students have successfully enrolled and are enjoying a fulfilling school life. I would like to improve my language skills in this a good environment for studying and work hard to acquire qualifications such as the English proficiency Test.
English Course teacher Hisaaki Kobayashi (Mathematics)
This course will help you to develop the communication, presentation, adaptability and imagination skills needed in an evolving global age. In class, you will spend time with international students from Asia and in the second year, you will study abroad to gain an international perspective. We will continue to focus on developing our students to become global leaders.

English Course teacher Elsayed Mounir (English)
